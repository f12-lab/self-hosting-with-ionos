OpenPiPy - Innovation in Sustainability and Digital Security

About OpenPiPy

OpenPiPy is a comprehensive technological solution designed to enhance home energy efficiency and strengthen digital security. Based on low-power hardware such as Raspberry Pi, OpenPiPy enables electricity consumption monitoring, IoT device automation, and home network protection.

Our Services

  • Energy consumption optimization through smart sensors.
  • Protection against digital threats via ad blockers and traffic filtering.
  • Home automation with integration into IoT platforms like Home Assistant.
  • Energy data analysis with personalized recommendations.

Competitive Advantages

OpenPiPy stands out from other solutions thanks to its focus on sustainability, privacy, and accessibility. By using open-source technology, we ensure transparency and customization of our solutions.

Business Model

OpenPiPy generates revenue through various sources, ensuring its sustainability and growth:

  • Specialized hardware sales: IoT sensor and controller kits.
  • Premium subscriptions: Advanced energy analysis and digital security services.
  • Technical support: Consulting and maintenance for business and individual clients.
